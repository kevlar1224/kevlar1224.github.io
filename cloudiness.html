Latitude Vs. Cloudiness

This plot shows the correlation between latitude and cloudiness. The distribution of data points shows that there is no relation between the two. This is to be expected, as while cloud coverage is affected by air temperature, it is also heavily influenced by topography, which varies independently of latitude.
